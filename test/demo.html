Badly Drawn Models Of Instagram

by Tom Hale | 5 days ago
facebookfacebook
twittertwitter
reddit
132 Shares

Instagram is the latest platform for those with social media clout to parade their beauty, riches and fame. Professional gurner Cara Delevingne has 9.5 million loyal pawns and full-time ‘nice face’ Miranda Kerr has 5.4 million minions. In homage to the cyber land of chiseled cheeks, perfect eyebrows and pouting selfies, there is an Instagram devoted to illustrations of models…. drawn really badly.

Taking off from the artistic legacy of Chris (Simpsons Artist), the page has almost a 100 different models portraitures, from famed names such as Kate Moss and Alexa Chung, to alt model doodle pads such as Rebecca Fox.

 

As if they stepped off their yacht in St Tropez to get a holiday caricature as a memento of their time, except it was done by a very stoned small child, the fine line drawings find quirks in the girls’ features and blow them up. A postmodern statement on the distorted image of fame and beauty in the social media age? Or just plain absurdity?

Badly Drawn Models is the brainchild of Sean Ryan. He also has tumblrs devoted to Badly Drawn Footballers, Badly Drawn Rappers, Badly Drawn Directors and Badly Drawn Authors.
